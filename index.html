HAND CRAFTED INSIDE + OUT

Olive Oil + Balsamic Vinegar Packaging

Labels and neck tags created for Lynmar Estate’s very limited production of estate-grown olive oil and balsamic vinegar. My illustrations were inspired by landmarks on the property, closely tied to the production of these offerings - reaffirming one of Lynmar’s core narratives of in-house craftsmanship. The scarcity of these bottlings was further depicted by hand-numbering each one.

Images property of Lynmar Estate Winery.

LYNMAR ESTATE'S RECOGNITION FOR LONG TERM ADVOCACY

Luxury wine producer Lynmar Estate Winery needed a special magnum design for wine club members who have held their subscription for five or more years. My hand-drawn illustration is a nod to Lynmar’s estate vineyard, Quail Hill Vineyard. The overall composition takes its shape from the number 5.
