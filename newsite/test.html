A VENOMOUS THREAD

THE HEARTBREAKERS by THE CLINK ROOM

Selected by The Clink Room to be made into a New Era 5950 fitted hat, the Heartbreakers is a mark I designed, inspired by last-place athletic teams that dash the chances of a rising team to see their season continue. A poised-to-strike cobra juxtaposed by a symbol of compassion created this emblematic garment.

COMMEMORATING COMMITMENT

LYNMAR ESTATE'S RECOGNITION FOR LONG TERM ADVOCACY

One of Lynmar Estate’s core values is community and rewarding the customers who have been loyal to their business and practices. In 2015, to recognize the most loyal customers, Lynmar Estate had a bottling of magnums to send to wine club members whose membership spanned five or more years. My illustration of the Quail is a nod to the estate’s vineyard, Quail Hill Vineyard. The composition is derived from the shape of a “5”.
